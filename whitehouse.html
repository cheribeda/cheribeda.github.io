White House Visitor Logs: Data Analysis & Visualization

This project analyzes and visualizes multi-year White House visitor data to explore patterns in guest frequency, appointment purposes, and seasonal visit trends. Python and data visualization tools were used to process and interpret over 1 million visit records.
