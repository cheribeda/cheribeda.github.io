Thynk Unlimited: A Netflix Horror Proposal

This project blends data science with creative strategy to pitch a new horror trilogy to Netflix. Using global viewership trends, Prophet forecasting, and visual storytelling, Thynk Unlimited explores how to make fear binge-worthy again.

🎬 Featured Movie Poster

📈 Key Features

  • Analyzed weekly Top 10 Netflix horror content globally
  • Identified viewing behavior patterns by country and genre
  • Created visualizations: bar charts, line graphs, heatmaps
  • Built forecasting models using Prophet
  • Developed a white paper as a Netflix proposal
